## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Crimson (#DC143C) to convey passion, energy, and expertise in QA/UX/UI.

- Accent color: Sky blue (#87CEEB) to highlight key interactive elements and call-to-action buttons, reflecting trustworthiness and innovation.
- Body font: 'Montserrat' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable text.
- Headline font: 'Poiret One' (serif) for card titles, to maintain design flair.
- Use Font Awesome icons for navigation and service cards; ensure consistency and clear representation of functions.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout for service cards, optimizing responsiveness and visual appeal on various devices.
- Subtle animations for button hovers and transitions to improve user experience and engagement.
